Rafting Site Plan

Author: Johnny Ortega

Overview

Purpose

To establish a robust online presence for our rafting business, ensuring that we effectively reach and engage our target audience, and ultimately drive increased bookings and revenue. This online presence will enable us to showcase the thrilling and safe rafting experiences we offer, build trust and credibility among potential customers, and provide essential information and resources to facilitate seamless trip planning and reservations. Through our online presence, we aim to expand our customer base, enhance customer satisfaction, and solidify our position as a premier destination for adventure enthusiasts seeking exhilarating rafting adventures.

Audience

Our primary target audience for the water rafting website consists of adventure-seeking individuals and groups aged 18 to 45, who are physically fit, have an affinity for outdoor activities, and are interested in experiencing the thrill of whitewater rafting in diverse natural settings.

Scenario Questions and Answers:

Scenario Question: What age group are we primarily targeting with our water rafting website?

Answer: Our primary target age group is 18 to 45 years old. This demographic typically seeks adventurous experiences and is physically fit enough to participate in whitewater rafting activities safely.

Scenario Question: What are the interests and characteristics of our target audience?

Answer: Our target audience comprises adventure enthusiasts who enjoy outdoor activities, nature exploration, and adrenaline-pumping experiences. They are often seeking novel challenges and memorable adventures to share with friends and family.

Scenario Question: How can we tailor our website content and messaging to appeal to our target audience effectively?

Answer: To resonate with our target audience, we should focus on highlighting the thrill and excitement of our rafting experiences, emphasize safety measures, provide detailed trip itineraries, and offer flexible booking options to cater to different group sizes and preferences. Additionally, we can showcase user-generated content, such as customer reviews and photos, to build trust and credibility among our audience.
